Kyrazo is a professional event orchestration platform designed to help teams reliably receive, process, and route events between systems. It acts as a central nervous system for your integrations, ensuring that every event reaches its destinationâ€”no matter what.

## Why Kyrazo?

Building a reliable webhook infrastructure is deceptively complex. As your system grows, you quickly encounter challenges that go far beyond a simple HTTP POST request.

### The Problem: Webhooks are Hard

Implementing webhooks manually often leads to several recurring pain points:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reliability & Retries" icon="rotate">
    Managing exponential backoff, jitter, and persistent delivery failures is a heavy engineering lift.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cryptographic Security" icon="shield-check">
    Generating and verifying webhook signatures correctly is critical but error-prone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Zero Observability" icon="eye-slash">
    When a delivery fails in a custom implementation, it often disappears into a black hole without logs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Developer Experience" icon="user-gear">
    Building internal tools to manage and monitor integration targets is an additional burden on your team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### The Solution: Kyrazo

Kyrazo abstracts this complexity into a single, high-performance API. By offloading your event delivery to Kyrazo, you gain:

- **Bulletproof Delivery**: Automatic retries with configurable policies.
- **Enterprise Security**: Standardized HMAC signatures and payload integrity checks.
- **Full Traceability**: Monitoring and detailed delivery logs for every event.
- **Ready-to-use Dashboard**: A professional interface to manage and monitor all your integration targets.
